You're out!




Information

After several complaints from tenants and upper management, you get a phone call from the board; You're out. Naturally, you get mad. That's when you decided to wreak havoc and kick out every last tenant in the building.
Of course, no one likes that, and people are calling the cops on you, so be fast and finish the work day! There's a single mother of two out there to evict!
(Also get a therapist dude. Like, seriously.)



Controls

  • Move – W, A, S, D
  • Look around - Mouse
  • Interact/Knock on doors – E
  • Show door list - Tab
  • Evict tenants – Type

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER:

If you find that you are unable to look around, please check your Google Chrome settings and check that mouse capturing is enabled for the site as the game needs this in order to work. Furthermore, some browsers don't support this for some reason, so if you still can't fix it download the Windows/Linux executable respectively on the Itch.io page. This should fix the issue.



Beginner's Jam Winter 2024

This game was made in 1 week for the Beginner's Jam Winter 2024, placed 3rd.
Play as an evil Landlord in this funny/parody typing game, and evict as many tenants from your apartment as possible!
